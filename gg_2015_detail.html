Category :Editorial, Visual Communication

Tools :Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop

Year :2015

Objective

Redesign the guide book and its development process based on feedback from the previous year.

Background

This guide book aimed for Japanese expats and tourists is published each year. On this occasion, the guide book from the previous year received negative feedback regarding the map and the development process of the book itself. A new map was created from scratch and a team of three including myself, analysed the feedback to design a better development process for the book.
