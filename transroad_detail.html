Objective

Develop a B2B service that helps businesses find a transportation company based on their shipment details.

Background

Since the mid-90's the number of transportation businesses increased from 40,000 to 60,000 in Japan, while total shipment volume decreased by a 30%. TransRoad's objective was to offer a service that will help businesses search for a transportation company that matched their shipment requirements.
